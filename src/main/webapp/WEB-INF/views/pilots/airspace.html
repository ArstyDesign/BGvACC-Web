Bulgarian airspace is mainly divided into two parts.

The western part is Sofia Control (LBSR_CTR), which covers central and western Bulgaria with the three major airports - Sofia (LBSF), Plovdiv (LBPD), Gorna Oryahovitsa (LBGO). This sector controls traffic from FL105 to FL660.

The frequency of this sector is 131.225

The eastern part is Varna Control (LBSR_V_CTR), which covers the eastern parts of Bulgaria, including the Black Sea coast as well as parts of the Black Sea. This includes the two major airports on the Black Sea - Varna (LBWN) and Burgas (LBBG). This sector controls traffic from FL105 to FL660.

The frequency of this sector is 134.700
